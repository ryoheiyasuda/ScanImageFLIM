Welcome to ScanImageFLIM GitHub Pages.

This software is a variant of ScanImage v.3.8 with FLIM functionality based on Matlab . It is currently compatible with PicoQuant TimeHarp 260 (N or P) and Becker and Hickl SPC-150 .

To install, please download ScanImage v 3.8.1 and then add/replace the file structure. Lisenced DLL and headers from the FLIM companies must be placed in the Matlab path.
